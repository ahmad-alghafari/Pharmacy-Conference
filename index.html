The 2nd Digital Pharmaceutical Innovations Conference, held on December 11-12, 2024, at the Intercontinental Hotel in Jeddah, was a remarkable event organized by the King Fahd Armed Forces Hospital. It aligned with Saudi Vision 2030 by focusing on digital transformation and innovation in healthcare. The conference successfully gathered experts to discuss cutting-edge research and innovative practices in pharmaceutical services.

The event featured a series of workshops and discussions on critical topics such as medication safety in the digital age, pharmacoeconomics, and the role of emotional intelligence in leadership. The conference provided educational value and commitment to advancing healthcare knowledge.

With a distinguished panel of speakers and attendees, the conference facilitated the exchange of insights and the development of advanced solutions, marking a significant step forward in the digital transformation of healthcare in Saudi Arabia. The success of the event was attributed to the collaborative efforts of the organizing and scientific committees, and the participation of leading professionals from various healthcare sectors.
